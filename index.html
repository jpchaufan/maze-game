Story
Instructions
You wake up, in a damp and dark place that is unfamiliar.The chilling image of the wretched sorcerer who trapped you in this tomb is still painfully ingrained in your mind.

You have nothing left to do but to make your way to the deepest depths, however deep they may be, and achieve your liberation from this mental prison.
Controls:
  • SPACE BAR for menu
  • ARROW KEYS or WASD to move
  • Select a potion and press Z to drink it
  • Select an item and press X to delete it
Gameplay Guide:
  • Defeat enemies to gain items and experience
  • Upgrade your skills and abilities
  • Manage which of your known abilities are available in battle in the skills menu
  • Note that you cannot take back any skill points or go back to a previous dungeon level, without loading a past saved game. (so save often!)
About Liberatus
Liberatus is an RPG game created just for fun. It was inspired by my favorite game series, the Diablo series, and by the pokemon gameboy games. Feel free to submit any questions or comments at my website
